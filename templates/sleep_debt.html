What is Sleep Debt?

Sleep debt is the difference between the ideal sleep your body needs and the sleep you actually get. Missing 1–2 hours each night quickly adds up to chronic sleep deprivation.

How to Repay Sleep Debt

  • Go to bed earlier for a few nights.
  • Take short 20–30 min naps.
  • Catch up gradually instead of oversleeping in one day.

Why Sleep Debt Matters

Chronic sleep debt can affect your:

  • Focus and memory
  • Weight management
  • Heart health
  • Immunity

FAQs

Q. What is sleep debt?
The difference between needed and actual sleep hours.

Q. How do you recover from sleep debt?
By sleeping earlier, taking short naps, and staying consistent with 8–9 hours.

Q. Is it possible to fully repay?
Yes, with consistent rest and sleep hygiene.
